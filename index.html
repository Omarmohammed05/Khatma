Core Features

Everything you need
to connect with the Quran.

Built for Muslim youth who want a focused, distraction-free experience.

📖
Uthmani Script
Authentic KFGQPC Uthmanic Hafs font across all 604 Madani Mushaf pages. Adjustable size for comfortable reading.
🎧
Audio Recitation
Stream from top reciters including Al-Afasy, Abdul Basit, and Al-Husary. Per-ayah repeat with custom range.
🌙
Prayer Times
Accurate prayer times and Qibla direction based on your real-time location. Hijri calendar included.
👥
Group Khatma
Invite up to 30 friends. Each takes a juz. Track everyone's progress in real-time until completion.
🌐
Offline First
All Quran text, translations (EN/FR), and Tafsir bundled locally. Read without internet, always.
✨
Ramadan Wrapped
A shareable stats card at the end of Ramadan showing your reading streak, pages, and completed khatmas.
Group Feature

Complete the Quran. Together.

Create a group, share a 4-digit code, and each member takes a juz. Watch the progress update in real-time until the final juz is complete.

Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Khatma ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Khatma mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and display name upon registration.
  • Reading progress: Current surah, ayah, and page numbers to sync progress via Supabase.
  • Location: Used temporarily for prayer times and Qibla direction (never stored).
  • Group data: Group names, member status, and completion progress.

8. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your personal data. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request full deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your reading statistics and history.

To exercise these rights, follow the instructions in the section below.

12. Account Deletion & Data Retention

Google Play policies require that we provide a path for you to delete your account even if you no longer have the app installed. If you wish to permanently delete your Khatma account and all associated data, please follow one of these methods:

Option A: Inside the App (Fastest)

Open Khatma → Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently erase your data from our servers.

Option B: Request via Email

If the app is uninstalled, click the button below to send us a deletion request from your registered email address.

Submit Deletion Request

Note: Account deletion is irreversible. Once deleted, your Khatma group historical progress and data cannot be restored.

11. Contact Us

Questions? Contact us at: support@khatma.app
