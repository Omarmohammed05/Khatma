Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Khatma ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Khatma mobile application ("App").

By using Khatma, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Reading progress: Current surah, ayah, and page number to sync your progress
  • Bookmarks: Saved ayahs and personal notes you create
  • Reading sessions: Duration and pages read per session for your personal stats
  • Location: Used only for prayer times and Qibla direction — never stored on our servers
  • Group data: Khatma group name, members, and juz completion status

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To sync your reading progress across devices
  • To enable group Khatma features with real-time updates
  • To generate your personal Ramadan Wrapped statistics
  • To send prayer time calculations (processed on-device)
  • To improve the App based on usage patterns

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row-Level Security (RLS) enabled. This means each user can only access their own data. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

The complete Quran text, translations, and Tafsir are bundled locally within the app — we never transmit Quran content data to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

Khatma uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database — privacy policy
  • Google Sign-In: Optional OAuth authentication — privacy policy
  • mp3quran.net: Quran audio streaming — no personal data shared
  • Aladhan API: Prayer times and Qibla — only coordinates sent, no account data

6. Location Data

We request location permission solely to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area. Your precise coordinates are:

  • Never stored on our servers
  • Only sent to Aladhan API to retrieve prayer time calculations
  • Never shared with advertisers or third parties for tracking

7. Children's Privacy

Khatma does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your reading data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below or use the "Delete Account" option in the App settings.

9. Advertising

Khatma contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not sell your data to advertisers. Our only monetization is a voluntary Pro subscription for advanced features.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through an in-app notification.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

team@khatma.qzz.io
